BAMPFA Email Signature Generator



Welcome to the BAMPFA Email Signature Generator. Follow the steps below to generate your new email signature, and add it to your bMail account.


The short version is: Fill out the form below, copy and paste the resulting content into your bMail signature field.


The long version is below.

1. Generate Signature

Fill out and submit this form. Submitting will open a new browser tab with your email signature. After submission, come back to this tab for further instructions.

Make sure all of your information is correct. If you made any mistakes, close the tab, and resubmit the form above with the corrected information.

Once the signature looks good:

  1. Go to the Edit dropdown menu and click "Select All"
  2. Go back to the Edit menu and select "Copy"

3. Paste signature into bMail

Sign in to bMail. Once you are in:

  1. Go to the gear icon in the corner and select the "settings" option
  2. Scroll down to the "Signature" field. Remove your old signature by highlighting everything in that field, and hitting delete
  3. Paste your new signature into the field. It should look like this:


  4. Assuming everything looks ok, scroll to the bottom of the page and press "Save Changes"

4. Test

Make sure your new signature looks good:

  1. Send yourself or a buddy an email
  2. Look at your new signature in that email and see how good it looks
  3. It looks so good
